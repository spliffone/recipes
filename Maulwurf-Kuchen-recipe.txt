 Cake mixture ingredients:
  - 300 g butter
  - 300 g flour
  - 250 g sugar
  - 5 eggs
  - 1 baking powder
  - 1 vanilla sugar
 
 Streusel ingredients:
  - 200 g flour
  - 125 g butter
  - 125 g sugar
  - 2 tablespoons cacao
 
 Directions:
 1. Put the cake mixture onto the griddle and form streusel and put them on dough.
 
 2. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
